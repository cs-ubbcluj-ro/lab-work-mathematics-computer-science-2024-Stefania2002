Statements:

The mini-language supports the following statements:

Assignment: Assigns the result of an expression to an identifier.

Example: IDENTIFIER := expression

Input/Output: Reads from or writes to standard input/output.

Example: READ(IDENTIFIER), WRITE(IDENTIFIER)

Conditional: Executes a statement based on a condition.

Example: IF condition THEN stmt [ELSE stmt]

Loop: Repeats a statement while a condition is true.

Example: WHILE condition DO stmt
